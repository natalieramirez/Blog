Ideas for when you're bored

Exercise

  • Challenge yourself to complete one of Chloe Ting's awesome workout programs.
  • Go for a run
    • I recommend checking out Nike Run Club. It has lots of great guided runs that make you look forward to your run.
    • Another great app to make running more fun is Zombies, Run! This app immerses you in a apocalyptic world where you have to outrun the zombies in order to survive. You listen to a mix of audio drama and pumping songs from your playlist.
  • Download Nike Training Club for access to lots of fun workouts. With Nike Training Club you are sure to find the perfect workouts that match your fitness level.
  • Try out a different type of exercise such as:
    • yoga
    • pilates
    • kickboxing
  • Go to your local field and run the track / practice a sport

Learn something new

There's so much out there to learn. All you have to do is pick something you are interested in and find a video, course, or website that will help you learn about your topic. Below are some links to great websites that can help you learn about whatever interests you.

  • Youtube
  • Coursera
  • Udemy
  • Khan Academy
